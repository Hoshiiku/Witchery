# Witchery
Witchery is a 2D-Game where you will embark on a journey full of adventures, stories, action and fun!

Meet _Name_! A witch whose magic ward was stolen and now it's left with nothing but her physical strength.

Your job here is helping her retrieve her magic ward, but be careful! This journey is no easy one, you'll have to fight monsters, explore dungeons and much more...


What are you waiting for? The journey ahead awaits...


_Game is currently under development. Final product may differ from early versions_
